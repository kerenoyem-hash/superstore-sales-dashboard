# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a retail Superstore sales dataset to evaluate sales performance, identify top-performing products, and understand revenue and profit trends across different regions and product categories.

The analysis was conducted using **Microsoft Excel**, where raw transactional data was cleaned, transformed, and analyzed using **Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and an interactive dashboard**.

The objective of this project is to transform raw data into clear business insights that support **data-driven decision making**.

---

## Dataset Source

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/aditirai2607/super-market-dataset

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains retail transaction records from a supermarket business.

### Key Fields

* **Order Date** – Date when the order was placed
* **Region** – Geographic sales region
* **Category** – Product category (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology)
* **Product Name** – Name of the product sold
* **Sales** – Revenue generated from each transaction
* **Profit** – Profit generated from each sale

These fields were used to analyze **sales performance, product demand, profitability, and sales trends over time**.

---

## Data Cleaning and Preparation

Before conducting the analysis, the dataset was cleaned and prepared to ensure accuracy and structure.

Steps performed:

* Checked for blank or missing values
* Checked for duplicate records
* Converted **Sales and Price columns** to currency format
* Converted **date columns** to proper date format
* Converted relevant fields to **number format**
* Structured the dataset as an **Excel Table**
* Verified consistency of data types across columns

These steps ensured that the dataset was **clean, organized, and ready for analysis**.

---

## Data Analysis Process

After cleaning the data, analysis was performed using **Excel Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts**.

### 1. Sales vs Profit Analysis

A combined column and line chart was created to compare:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit

Across different **product categories and regions**, helping identify which categories generate the most revenue and profit.

### 2. Sales Trend Over Time

A **line chart** was created to analyze sales trends by order date.

This helps identify:

* Seasonal sales patterns
* Growth trends
* High-performing quarters

### 3. Top Products by Sales

A Pivot Table was used to identify the **Top 10 products by total sales**.

For dashboard clarity, only the **Top 5 products** were displayed to highlight the most impactful revenue-generating products.

## Dashboard Design

An interactive **Excel dashboard** was created to present insights clearly and visually.

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Sales:** $2,326,534.35
* **Total Profit:** $292,296.81

These KPIs provide a quick overview of **overall business performance**.

### Interactive Filters (Slicers)

Users can filter the dashboard by:

* Product Category
* Region

This allows dynamic exploration of **sales performance across business segments**.

### Visualizations Included

* Sales vs Profit comparison chart
* Sales trend over time chart
* Top performing products table
* KPI summary cards
* Interactive slicers

---

## Key Business Insights

### Technology Category Generates the Highest Revenue

Technology products generate the highest sales, indicating strong demand in this category.

### Sales Peak in the Fourth Quarter

Sales consistently increase during **Q4**, indicating strong seasonal demand toward the end of the year.

### A Small Number of Products Drive Most Revenue

Top-performing products contribute significantly to total sales, highlighting the importance of focusing on **best-selling items**.
